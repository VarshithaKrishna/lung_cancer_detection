About Large Cell Carcinoma

Causes

Heavy smoking, asbestos exposure, and rapid genetic mutations in lung cells.

Concerns

Large cell carcinoma is aggressive, grows quickly, and often spreads to other organs early.

Next Steps

Immediate oncology consultation is recommended. Treatment options include chemotherapy, radiation, and targeted therapy.

Precautionary Measures

Quit smoking, reduce exposure to harmful chemicals, and undergo regular medical checkups.
