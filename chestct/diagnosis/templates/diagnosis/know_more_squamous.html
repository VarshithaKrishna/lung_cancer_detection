About Squamous Cell Carcinoma

Causes

Strongly linked to heavy smoking, chronic lung irritation, and long-term exposure to carcinogens.

Concerns

Squamous cell carcinoma usually appears in the central parts of the lungs and can obstruct airways.

Next Steps

Bronchoscopy and biopsy are often required. Treatment may involve surgery, chemotherapy, or targeted therapy.

Precautionary Measures

Quit smoking, monitor respiratory health, and seek medical advice if persistent cough or chest pain occurs.
