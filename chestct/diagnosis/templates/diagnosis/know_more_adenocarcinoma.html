About Adenocarcinoma

Causes

Smoking, prolonged exposure to pollutants, genetic predisposition, and occupational hazards.

Concerns

Adenocarcinoma often develops in the outer regions of the lungs and may spread before symptoms appear.

Next Steps

Consult a pulmonologist or oncologist. Recommended tests include biopsy, PET scans, and further imaging.

Precautionary Measures

Quit smoking, avoid secondhand smoke, maintain a healthy diet, and schedule regular screenings.
